Durable materials

All EcoCabins have a high insulation value, are produced with
sustainable and / or natural materials where possible and
have the "round corners" as a striking external characteristic,
made possible by the specific use of materials. The
advantages of building with wood are many: wood is light,
flexible, insulates well and builds quickly and efficiently.
